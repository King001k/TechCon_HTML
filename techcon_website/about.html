Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local event to connect developers and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for sharing knowledge, launching products, and shaping the future of tech.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to inspiring innovation, building community, and driving impact through technology. We aim to empower diverse voices and foster collaboration among tech professionals worldwide.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace – Renowned for her foundational work in computational theory and ethics in AI. Delivered a keynote on responsible AI design in 2022.

Elon Turing – A leading figure in quantum computing and founder of QubitNet. Spoke about the future of computing at TechCon 2023.

Grace Hopper Jr. – Promoter of open-source collaboration. Shared insights on building inclusive developer communities.
